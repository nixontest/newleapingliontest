Submissions

Calling All Writers!

Leaping Lion Books accepts unsolicited manuscripts

Do you want to express your unique style or story through words? Do you have a working manuscript? Do you have dreams for it to be published? You should pitch it to Leaping Lion Books! Please visit our contact page for information on where to submit your pitch.

Leaping Lion Books at York University is looking for inspiring and interesting manuscript submissions. Your submission could be seen by Mike O'Connor, the course director of the Book Publishing Practicum. If accepted for publication, it will be handed over to practicum students to be, among other tasks, edited, marketed, designed and then published by Leaping Loin Books. You can submit a fiction or non-fiction manuscript but be ready to take it through to maturity with students passionate about learning the intricacies of the book publishing industry.

Things to keep in mind before submitting your manuscript

1. We hold a track record for debuting many authors. Currently, Leaping Lion Books is working with Professor Arthur Haberman on his first mystery novel, Wild Justice. Although a former publication is an asset, it is not a requirement.

2. Contact us with a query first: Send us a short pitch before sending along your full manuscript.

3. Check out our books page for a taste of what we have published in the past. Our former publications include full-length fiction and non-fiction such as Nic Labriola’s short story collection, Francis and the Animals (2013), Donna Jean MacKinnon’s non-fiction book, Newsgirls (2017), Bruce Jay Friedman’s collected plays, 3.1 Plays (2012) and many others.
