About Us

Leaping Lion Books is an independent micro-publishing press at York University in Toronto, Canada, managed by York’s very own fourth-year Professional Writing students. With limited guidance from Course Director Mike O’Connor, students transform a manuscript into the real thing—a published book! They edit, design, produce and market it; They even get to make a kick-ass website like this one! For more information on each department and their responsibilities, visit Careers, or to see the greatest, most uuhhmazing students ever, check out the team page.

We pride ourselves in publishing avid Canadian authors (because the world needs more Canada) and we accept manuscripts from all genres except for romance and poetry (sorry, Romantics!). In the past, we’ve published mystery, plays, history, short story anthologies and various non-fiction. Visit Submissions for more information.

Leaping Lion was launched in 2009 by Geoffrey Huck, an Associate Professor in the Writing Department. He wanted to give students an inside look and a hands-on learning experience of the real-life workings of a publishing house. Although we lack years of industry experience, we make up for it with our millennial enthusiasm, perseverance and creativity. Our strength comes from both the vigorous teamwork and diverse individuality of our students.
