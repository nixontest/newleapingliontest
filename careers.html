Careers

Come join Leaping Lion Books!

Leaping Lion Books, which works in conjunction with WRIT 4004 6.0 Book Publishing Practicum, is a student run book publishing company. We focus mainly on trade books and highlight experiential learning in the book making process. As an ’employee’ of Leaping Lion Books you can choose from an array of positions and work alongside other students who are passionate about a future in book publishing.

Some Positions Available

Publisher : You get to supervise the whole publishing firm (no pressure).

Associate Publisher: You get to work alongside the publisher to ensure the tasks of the publishing firm run smoothly. To do this you are responsible for communicating with directors in the many departments of the firm.

Editorial Director : You get to work with the assistant editorial directors and copyeditors to bring a manuscript to maturity.

Production Director: You get to work with the assistant production directors and the production editors, as well as the editorial and design department, to create the physical and visual aspects of the book.

Design Director: You get to work with the assistant design director, photo editors and designer to prepare internal and external cover design and any illustrations.

Marketing Director: Among other tasks, you get to work with the marketing department on pre- and post-publication advertising and publicity.

Rights and Permission Director: Among other tasks, you get to work with the assistant rights & permissions director and Rights & Permissions Manager to look over the manuscript for any copyright issues.

Business Director: You get to work with the assistant business directors and Business Accounting Manager to oversee the budget.

Web Director : You get to work with the Web Designers to contribute to or improve the Leaping Lion Books website.
