Dancing With Ghosts

Emily Gillespie


University, freshman year was supposed to mean freedom. For Patricia, it was supposed to mean her escape from parents who didn’t understand her, who pushed her away every time she reached out for help. Now, a new city, new school, new friends, and a fresh start was waiting for her - wasn’t that how it was supposed to work? Instead, when Patricia moves from her small isolated hometown to the big city in Toronto, she finds herself more alone than ever. That’s until she meets Derek, an intriguing and mysterious classmate, she’s instantly drawn to by his worldly knowledge and charm. For a while, things are smooth sailing between them. For a while, it’s thrilling being invited into a world unlike anything Patricia has ever experienced before. But, this isn’t a love story, and not everyone is who they seem.
