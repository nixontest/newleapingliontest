Danny's Latkes from Wild Justice



Ingredients

2 large Russet potatoes, grated
1 small onion, grated
1 large egg, beaten
3 tbsp flour
1 1/2 tsp Kosher salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1/4 cup peanut oil for frying

Equipment

2 large bowls
A non-stick pan or skillet

Instructions

1. Put grated potatoes in a large bowl, and fill the bowl with water. Drain water. Repeat step three times or until the water is mostly clear.

2. Squeeze potatoes in your hands or through a cheesecloth to remove water from the potatoes. Place squeezed potatoes in a clean bowl.

3. Mix squeezed potatoes with onion, egg, flour, salt and pepper.

4. Put oil into a the pan and heat on medium-high heat until the oil is shimmering but not smoking.

5. Place two tablespoons of the mixture into the skillet in a round latke shape. Keep placing latkes with ample space between each latke. Do not overcrowd the pan.

6. Flip latkes once they are golden brown on the bottom side, and fry the other side (about 5 minutes per side).

7. Once the latkes are finished, remove latkes from the oil and place them on a paper tower to remove excess oil.

8. Serve immediately with apple sauce or sour cream or freeze for later use. Enjoy!
