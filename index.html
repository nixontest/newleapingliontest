Deep in the city, there's a mystery.

Wild Justice

In late fall, Homicide Detective Sergeant Daniel Miller arrives at Kenneth Avenue ready to tackle a new case. But, he finds a strange death in the Yonge and Sheppard high-rise. As he and Detective Constable Nadiri Rahimi explore a number of killings in the Toronto Area they find there is more than one thread to the story. In their investigations they take us from The Scarborough Bluffs to The University of Toronto to CAMH centres to The Danforth hoping to find unexpected killers.
